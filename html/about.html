Who we are

Smarthouse is a pioneer in enhancing the mundane aspects of life. Our mission is to innovate and create technological solutions that simplify daily life while fostering deeper connections and facilitating growth in an ever-evolving digital world.

History

In 2024, Minh, alongside Bao and Vinh, set forth on a transformative journey, founding Smarthouse with a clear mission: to innovate and simplify daily life in the digital age. From humble beginnings, our passion for technological excellence propelled us forward, earning recognition for our forward-thinking approach.
Throughout our history, we've embraced change, adapting to emerging trends and technologies while staying true to our core values. Guided by our mission, every endeavor at Smarthouse is infused with purpose, aiming to simplify, connect, and empower in an ever-evolving digital world.
As we look ahead, our journey continues, fueled by innovation and driven by purpose. Together, let's shape tomorrow's technology and transform lives for the better.
