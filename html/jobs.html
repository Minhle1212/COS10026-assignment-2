Data Engineer

Job brief

You will use various methods to transform raw data into useful data systems. Overall, you will strive for efficiency by aligning data systems with business goals. Data engineer skills also include familiarity with several programming languages and knowledge of learning machine methods.

Key responsibilities

  • Analyze and organize raw data
  • Build data systems and pipelines
  • Evaluate business needs and objectives
  • Interpret trends and patterns
  • Conduct complex data analysis and report on results
  • Prepare data for prescriptive and predictive modeling
  • Build algorithms and prototypes

Skills and qualifications

Essentials

  • Previous experience as a data engineer or in a similar role
  • Technical expertise with data models, data mining, and segmentation techniques
  • Knowledge of programming languages (e.g. Java and Python)
  • Hands-on experience with SQL database design

Preferable

  • Great numerical and analytical skills
  • Degree in Computer Science, IT, or similar field; a Master degree is a plus
  • Data engineering certification (e.g IBM Certified Data Engineer)

Software Engineer

Job brief

Builds financial technology products and services to meet the needs of Smarthouse and our clients. Develops software using a host of languages and open source technologies including Java, Python, JavaScript, C#, Cassandra, Angular, React Kubernetes, Kafka, Docker, Solr, iOS and more.

Key responsibilities

  • Takes ownership of projects and watch your creations be released to thousands of users.
  • Works closely with senior engineers developing specific parts of the Smarthouse platforms
  • Collaborates with users and the product team to design new features.
  • Gains a broad understanding of the infrastructure and the capabilities of Smarthouse
  • Collaborates with colleagues to build innovative products during firm-wide hackathons.

Skills and qualifications

Essentials

  • 5 years of experience as a Software Engineering, Software Development Engineer, Software Engineer, or related role.
  • Experience with: Data structures and algorithms; Applying design patterns,Angular, Spring, SQL, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.
  • Hands-on experience with building distributed systems and storage technologies such as Cassandra, Redis, and relational database.

Preferable

  • 2 years of experience with: C# Programming language.
  • Degree in Computer Science, IT, or similar field; a Master degree is a plus
  • Experience with: Modern web application development, and Scala, Apache Spark, Azure Data Factory, Power BI.
